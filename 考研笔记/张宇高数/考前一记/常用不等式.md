1.当 $x \in (0,\frac{\pi}{2})$ 时
$$\begin{align}
    \frac{2}{\pi} x<\sin x <x \\
    \sin x \leq x \leq \tan x
\end{align}$$

2.当 $x >0$ 时
$$\begin{align}
    \frac{x}{x + 1} <\ln (1 + x) < x
\end{align}$$

3.当 $x > 0$ 时
$$\begin{align}
    e^x \geq x + 1
\end{align}$$

4.当 $x,y>0$ 时
$$\begin{align}
    x + y \geq 2\sqrt{xy}
\end{align}$$

当 $x_i >0 , i=1,2\cdots n$ ,则
$$\begin{align}
    x_1 + x_2 \cdots + x_n \geq n \sqrt[n]{x_1x_2\cdots x_n}
\end{align}$$
